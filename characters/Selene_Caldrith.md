# Lady Selene Caldrith

**Full Name:** Lady Selene Caldrith  
**Titles:** Younger full sister of Lucian Caldrith

**Personality:** Warm, bright, and adventurous. The youngest child of the family, she brings energy and lightness to the often serious Caldrith household. She loves exploring and regularly nags her older siblings (especially Lucian and Lyra) to take her out for fun and adventures.

**Quirks:** Quite clumsy and has shown little talent for magic, which sometimes frustrates her but doesn’t dim her spirit.

**Relationship with Lucian:** Looks up to him and feels safe with him. Her affection helps bring out his warmer side.

**Role in Story:** Provides emotional lightness and humanization to the Caldrith family. A source of joy and occasional comic relief.
