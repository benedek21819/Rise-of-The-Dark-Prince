# Lord Kael and Lord Thorne Caldrith

**Full Name:** Lord Kael Caldrith and Lord Thorne Caldrith  
**Titles:** Younger half-brothers of Lucian Caldrith (twins)

**Personality:** Strong, straightforward “musclehead” types. They have extraordinary talent in combat and impressive physiques but show little aptitude for magic or complex strategy.

**Role in Story:** Loyal and respectful toward Lucian as the future head of the family. They train hard to become his strong physical support and protectors. As twins, they are nearly inseparable and often act as a unit.
