# Princess Seren Kaelarion

**Full Name:** Princess Seren Kaelarion  
**Aliases / Titles:** The Sun Princess, Daughter of King Aldric Kaelarion  
**Age:** 3 years younger than Lucian Caldrith

**Appearance:** Extremely beautiful with long, blonde-almost white hair and bright eyes. She has a warm, radiant presence that matches her nickname. Her style is elegant and soft, often incorporating the royal colors of deep crimson and gold.

**Personality:** The Genuine Sun — warm, optimistic, open, and sincerely kind. Her love for Lucian is pure and not calculated. She is intelligent but not particularly ambitious or political. She believes love and goodwill can bridge the gap between their houses.

**Strengths:** Empathy, emotional intelligence, ability to inspire loyalty in commoners, natural warmth that draws people to her.
**Flaws:** Somewhat naïve about the darker side of politics and Lucian’s cold nature. Can be overly trusting.

**Abilities:** Moderate talent in healing magic.

**Goals:**
- Win Lucian’s genuine love and affection
- Create a peaceful and lasting alliance between House Kaelarion and House Caldrith

**Key Relationships:**
- **Lucian Caldrith** (fiancé): Deeply in love with him and actively trying to win his favor, despite his emotional distance.
- **Crown Prince Dorian Kaelarion** (older brother): Protective of him despite his many flaws and ambitions.
- **King Aldric Kaelarion** (father): Close and loving daughter. She shares a warm bond with him.

**Role in Story:** Emotional contrast to Lucian. She represents light, warmth, and sincerity against his coldness and growing darkness. Her persistence may gradually soften him — or lead to deep personal pain if he continues down a darker path.
