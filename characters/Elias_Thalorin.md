# Lord Elias Thalorin

**Full Name:** Lord Elias Thalorin  
**Titles:** Heir / rising political face of House Thalorin  
**Affiliation:** House Thalorin  
**Relation:** Brother of Lady Elara Caldarion; maternal uncle of Lucian and Selene  
**Status:** Alive

**Concept:** Political face of the next Thalorin generation  
More publicly active and alliance-conscious than a pure healer role would suggest. He wants the Caldarion connection maintained and used wisely for Thalorin’s advantage.

**Appearance:**  
Polished and presentable in the Thalorin style — elegant rather than severe. Carries himself like a man prepared for courts and negotiation more than open battlefields.

**Personality:**  
Capable, ambitious within soft-power bounds, and socially adept. He respects Caldarion strength while remaining aware that Thalorin cannot and should not compete with it militarily. Protective of family prestige and attentive to how Lucian’s rise may affect both houses.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Lord Halric Thalorin**: Father and current head; Elias is being shaped as his successor in public and political matters.
- **Lady Ysara Thalorin**: Mother.
- **Lady Elara Caldarion**: Sister. Values the bridge she created into House Caldarion.
- **Lucian Caldarion**: Nephew and critical alliance figure. Elias is likely to treat him with careful respect and interest.

**Role in Story:**  
Maternal uncle and the active political link to House Thalorin. Useful for alliance maintenance, court-facing Thalorin business, and soft pressure or support around Elara’s children.
