# Lady Ysara Thalorin

**Full Name:** Lady Ysara Thalorin  
**Titles:** Lady of House Thalorin  
**Affiliation:** House Thalorin  
**Relation:** Mother of Lady Elara Caldarion; maternal grandmother of Lucian and Selene  
**Status:** Alive

**Concept:** Warmer healer figure  
The more openly compassionate center of Elara’s birth family. Her values — care, healing, and emotional presence — strongly shaped Elara and stand in clear contrast to the severity of Blackspire.

**Appearance:**  
Graceful and composed, with a softer presence than Caldarion women. Favors the lighter Thalorin palette. Her manner is gentle without being fragile.

**Personality:**  
Warm, attentive, and principled in a quieter way than court politicians. She prioritizes well-being and human cost more readily than Halric. Proud of Elara, protective of her grandchildren, and less comfortable with pure dynastic coldness.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Lord Halric Thalorin**: Husband; pragmatic counterpart to her warmer instincts.
- **Lady Elara Caldarion**: Daughter. Deep emotional bond. Ysara is one of the clearest sources of the openness Elara brought into House Caldarion.
- **Lord Elias Thalorin**: Son.
- **Lucian and Selene**: Grandchildren she regards with genuine affection and concern, especially regarding the emotional climate of Blackspire.

**Role in Story:**  
Maternal-line grandmother and emotional root of Elara’s warmth. Useful for contrast scenes, family visits, and reminding Lucian that not all power houses are built on cold severity.
