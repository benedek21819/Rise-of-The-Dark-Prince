# Lady Selene Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lady Selene Caldarion  
**Titles:** Youngest child of Lord Vesper Caldarion and Lady Elara, full younger sister of Lucian  
**Birth Year:** 1043 IE  
**Age:** 5 years younger than Lucian (12 years old in present day ≈ 1055 IE)  
**Status:** Alive

**Appearance:**  
She has a brighter, softer presence than most of her siblings and takes more after her mother, Lady Elara. She has dark hair and very light grey eyes that can appear blue depending on the lighting. Her features are still youthful and open. She is smaller in stature and often looks a bit disheveled from running around or exploring, despite Lyra’s efforts to keep her presentable.

**Personality:**  
Warm, bright, and open-hearted. She has an adventurous spirit and loves to explore the halls, gardens, and surrounding areas of Blackspire Keep. She regularly nags her older siblings (especially Lucian, Lyra, and the twins) to take her out or spend time with her. She is clumsy and frequently trips, drops things, or causes small accidents without meaning any harm. She shows little natural talent for magic or serious combat training. Emotionally expressive and easy to read, she is in many ways the opposite of Lucian’s controlled coldness. She brings a rare sense of normal childhood energy into the otherwise heavy and disciplined atmosphere of House Caldarion.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Lucian Caldarion**: She adores her older brother and is not intimidated by his cold reputation. One of the few people who can still draw mild exasperation or rare soft amusement from him. Lucian is quietly protective of her.
- Close with **Lyra**, who often tries (with mixed success) to keep her presentable and safe.
- Gets along well with the twins, Riven and Thorne, who sometimes indulge her requests for adventure.
- Daughter of **Lady Elara** (main wife) and **Lord Vesper**.

**Strengths:**
- Emotional warmth and sincerity
- High energy and resilience
- Ability to lighten the mood of those around her
- Genuine affection that softens even colder family members

**Flaws:**
- Clumsy and sometimes reckless in her curiosity
- Lacks talent in the areas House Caldarion values most (combat and magic)
- Can be naive about the darker realities of their world and politics

**Role in Story:**  
Selene represents innocence and emotional light within the Caldarion household. She serves as a living reminder of what Lucian still has to protect. Her presence creates natural moments of warmth, humor, and contrast against the darker, colder elements of the story and Lucian’s personality.
