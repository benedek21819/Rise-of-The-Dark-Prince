# Lady Selene Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lady Selene Caldarion  
**Titles:** Younger full sister of Lucian  
**Status:** Alive (youngest child of the main wife, Lady Elara)

**Appearance:** Warm and bright presence.

**Personality:** Warm and bright personality. Adventurous and likes to explore. Regularly nags her older siblings to take her out and have fun. She is clumsy and does not show much talent for magic.

**Key Relationships:**
- Close with her older siblings (especially Lucian and Lyra).
- Daughter of Lady Elara and Lord Vesper.

**Role in Story:** Brings lightness, adventure, and emotional warmth to the otherwise serious and cold House Caldarion. She often serves as comic relief or a reminder of innocence and family bonds for Lucian.
