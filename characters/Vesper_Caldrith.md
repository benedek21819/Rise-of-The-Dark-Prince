# Lord Vesper Caldrith

**Full Name:** Lord Vesper Caldrith  
**Titles:** Head of House Caldrith, Lord of the Caldrith Duchy

**Appearance:** Tall and imposing with silver-streaked black hair, a scarred face or hands from years of battle, and piercing dark eyes. Always dressed in black and silver with an aura of quiet authority and weariness.

**Personality:** The Calculated Veteran — calm, strategic, emotionally reserved, and battle-scarred. He views everything through the lens of House Caldrith’s long-term survival and power. He is pragmatic and honor-bound but willing to be ruthless when necessary.

**Strengths:** Strategic genius, immense Will, deep bond with Lirath, unwavering loyalty to House Caldrith.
**Flaws:** Emotional distance, obsession with secrecy, tendency to view people (including family) as tools.

**Abilities:** Bonded to the silver dragon **Lirath**. Highly skilled in military strategy and Veilweaving (likely as a Magic Knight).

**Role in Story:** Mentor figure and current patriarch. He pushes Lucian hard while trying to maintain the house’s reclusive power.
