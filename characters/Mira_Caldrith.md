# Lady Mira Caldrith

**Full Name:** Lady Mira Caldrith  
**Titles:** First Concubine of Lord Vesper Caldrith, Mother of Lady Lyra

**Personality:** The Elegant Loyalist — graceful, charming, and focused on family harmony and proper upbringing. She accepts her position with quiet elegance and raises her daughter Lyra to be refined and useful to the house.

**Appearance:** Striking beauty with long dark hair (with subtle silver highlights), elegant features, and graceful posture. Dresses in sophisticated black and silver with decorative touches.

**Role in Story:** Provides a softer, more cultured influence in the family while helping maintain internal harmony.
