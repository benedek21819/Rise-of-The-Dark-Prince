# Lady Elara Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lady Elara Caldarion  
**Titles:** Main Wife of Lord Vesper Caldarion, Mother of Lucian and Selene  
**Status:** Alive

**Appearance:** Bright silver hair (striking against the dark aesthetic of House Caldarion). She has an elegant, refined presence and shows overt affection toward her children.

**Personality:** Warm and openly affectionate with her children (especially compared to the more reserved Vesper). She balances the cold, strategic nature of the house with genuine maternal care. She is proud of her family and the legacy of House Caldarion but can be more emotional and protective than her husband.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Husband:** Lord Vesper Caldarion (current head of house)
- **Children:** Lucian (eldest son and heir), Selene (younger daughter), and step-children from the concubines
- Strong, overt affection toward her biological children

**Role in Story:** Provides emotional warmth and maternal perspective within the otherwise cold and strategic House Caldarion. She humanizes the family dynamic and may serve as a source of comfort or conflict for Lucian as he navigates his duties and emotions.
