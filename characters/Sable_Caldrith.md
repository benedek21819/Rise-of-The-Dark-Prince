# Lady Sable Caldrith

**Full Name:** Lady Sable Caldrith  
**Titles:** Second Concubine of Lord Vesper Caldrith, Mother of Lord Kael and Thorne

**Backstory:** Born to a minor military family that was nearly wiped out during the Last Continental War. She survived as a young girl through resilience and was later noticed by Lord Vesper for her natural beauty and quiet strength. Brought into House Caldrith as a concubine to strengthen ties with loyal military bloodlines.

**Personality:** The Warm Survivor — kind-hearted, nurturing, and pragmatic. Less refined but naturally beautiful. Focuses on raising strong, loyal sons.

**Appearance:** Striking, earthy beauty with a practical and bold style.

**Role in Story:** Provides raw strength and loyalty through her twin sons, who serve as protectors for Lucian.
