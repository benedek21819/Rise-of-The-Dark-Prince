# Lucian Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lucian Caldarion  
**Aliases / Titles:** Heir of House Caldarion, the Dark Prince  
**Nickname Origin:** "Dark Prince" — Given because he almost always wears full black clothing and possesses a very calm, calculating, and sometimes cold personality.

**Age:** [Start age + how it changes over the story]  
**House & Position:** Eldest son and direct heir of **House Caldarion**, one of the eight Great Houses. His house is the most powerful military force in the kingdom, helped place the current royal dynasty on the throne, but remains highly reclusive and rarely interferes in kingdom affairs.

**Appearance:** Lucian Caldarion is tall with a commanding yet graceful presence. He has long, straight black hair that often falls loosely around his shoulders or is tied back with simple silver clasps. His skin is clear and strikingly pale, almost porcelain-like. His most distinctive feature is his eyes — a very dark, intense purple that can appear nearly black in certain lighting, giving him a piercing and unsettling gaze. He is widely regarded as one of the most handsome young men on the continent, with refined, aristocratic features that blend cold beauty with quiet authority. He almost always dresses in full black clothing, often accented with subtle silver embroidery or accessories that reflect House Caldarion’s colors and sigil (a silver sword with black wings). His overall aura is refined, intimidating, and coldly beautiful.

**Personality:** Lucian presents a calm, calculating, and often cold demeanor to the outside world and most people. He is emotionally distant and can be almost cruel in his pragmatism toward strangers or political rivals. However, toward the very few people he truly cares about (his inner circle), he is deeply loving, protective, and caring — almost to an extreme degree. This creates a strong emotional imbalance: he is capable of infinite warmth and loyalty toward those close to him, while remaining coldly detached or even ruthless toward everyone else. He speaks in a measured, articulate, and often reserved manner. He rarely shows strong emotions publicly, but those who know him well can sometimes catch rare moments of genuine warmth or quiet protectiveness.

**Strengths:** Remarkable self-control, sharp strategic mind, deep loyalty and protectiveness toward his inner circle, ability to remain objective when dealing with outsiders.  
**Fatal Flaw:** His extreme emotional imbalance can lead to blind spots. He may go to ruthless or morally questionable lengths to protect the few people he loves, and his cold treatment of outsiders can isolate him or create unnecessary enemies. He struggles with balancing his deep care for his inner circle against the greater good or political necessity.  
**Quirks/Habits:** Often observes others in silence before speaking. Tends to touch or adjust the silver clasps in his hair when deep in thought. Rarely shows open affection except in private with those he trusts. Has a habit of giving people unsettlingly direct eye contact.

**Abilities / Skills / Powers:** Exceptional military training and leadership. Bonded since birth to **Ancalion**, the youngest dragon owned by House Caldarion. Ancalion has completely black scales, pitch black flames, deep dark purple eyes, and an exceptionally large wingspan that makes him very fast. Due to their strong bond (formed through the cradling ritual), they can sense each other’s emotions. Ancalion’s personality is notably similar to Lucian’s. The dragon’s egg was laid by **Lirath** (House Caldarion’s publicly known female dragon). Lucian is pursuing the **hybrid path** (Magic Knight + Mage) and has latent ability for **Commandments**.

**Character Arc:** Starts as the respected (and somewhat feared) heir of House Caldarion → faces threats to his position and family → turns to darkness to protect or surpass what he was born into → confronts the cost of the power he gains and how it changes him.  
**Key Relationships:** Father (current head of house — bonded to **Lirath**), younger siblings (potential rivals or supporters), **Princess Seren Kaelarion** (initially his distant fiancée. Over time they become close; Lucian eventually accepts her and decides he will marry her. By the time the Sisters of the Current arrive to claim her as a Veilseer, Seren is no longer merely his betrothed — she is one of his people. When the Sisters formally request custody, Lucian rejects the request entirely. His position is simple: Seren is his future wife. Nobody will take her. This intervention mirrors an ancient Caldarion action on behalf of a previous Kaelarion Veilseer). The Royal Family (strong political alliance through the engagement), other Great Houses (who view his family with a mix of respect and wariness).  
**Role in Story:** Central protagonist whose rise (and moral descent) drives the narrative. Morally ambiguous hero/villain depending on perspective. His reclusive house background and "Dark Prince" reputation make his journey particularly compelling. His decisive rejection of the Sisters’ claim on Seren marks a key early assertion of his protectiveness and begins the re-opening of contact between Kaelor and the mainland.
