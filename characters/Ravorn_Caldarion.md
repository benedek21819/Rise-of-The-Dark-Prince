# Lord Ravorn Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lord Ravorn Caldarion  
**Titles:** Previous Head of House Caldarion, Last Rider of Morvath  
**Status:** Deceased (died during the Last Continental War)

**Appearance:** Tall and muscular with a powerful warrior’s build. He bore many scars from numerous battles. He had full black hair and dark, intense eyes. Even in death, he is remembered as an imposing and commanding figure.

**Personality:** The Legendary Warrior-Brother — a powerful, charismatic, and battle-hardened leader who valued loyalty and personal bonds above pure politics. His friendship with the father of King Aldric Kaelarion was genuine and deep, almost like that of sworn brothers. He was fierce in war but capable of great loyalty and warmth toward those he trusted. His presence inspired absolute loyalty in those who followed him.

**Strengths:** Exceptional combat skill, mastery of dragon-riding (especially with Morvath), ability to inspire absolute loyalty, charismatic leadership.
**Flaws:** Could be reckless when protecting those he cared about. His death left a heavy legacy and created the need for secrecy around Morvath.

**Abilities:** Last bonded rider of the hidden dragon **Morvath**. One of the most formidable warriors and dragon riders of his generation.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Father of King Aldric Kaelarion**: Shared a deep, brother-like friendship. Together they reinforced the long-standing alliance between House Caldarion and House Kaelarion that helped secure the current royal dynasty’s rise to power.
- **Lord Vesper Caldarion** (son): Raised him with high expectations. Vesper continues to honor the alliance and legacy his father built.
- **Morvath**: His bonded dragon. After Ravorn’s death in the Last Continental War, it was publicly claimed that Morvath died with him. In reality, the dragon survived and has been kept hidden by House Caldarion ever since.

**Legacy:**
Ravorn is remembered as a legendary figure whose personal bond with the previous generation of House Kaelarion reinforced the foundation of the current political order. His death in the Last Continental War is both a source of pride and a heavy burden for House Caldarion. Lucian grows up in the shadow of this legendary grandfather.

**Role in Story:** A legendary previous-generation figure whose actions and death still shape the present. His brother-like friendship with Aldric’s father gives emotional and historical weight to the current alliance, while the secrecy surrounding Morvath’s survival remains one of House Caldarion’s greatest secrets.
