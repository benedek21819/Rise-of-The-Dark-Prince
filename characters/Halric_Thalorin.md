# Lord Halric Thalorin

**Full Name:** Lord Halric Thalorin  
**Titles:** Head of House Thalorin  
**Affiliation:** House Thalorin  
**Relation:** Father of Lady Elara Caldarion; maternal grandfather of Lucian and Selene  
**Status:** Alive

**Concept:** Pragmatic head of a soft-power house  
A careful, prestige-minded lord who understands that House Thalorin’s strength lies in healing, necessity, and influence rather than open military dominance. He supported the Caldarion marriage as a major elevation of Thalorin standing and protection.

**Appearance:**  
Refined rather than martial. Neatly kept appearance, softer color palette than Caldarion black-and-silver — whites, soft blues, and silver accents. Composed posture and a controlled, deliberate manner.

**Personality:**  
Pragmatic, courteous, and politically alert. He values reputation, usefulness, and long-term advantage. Not cold in the Caldarion sense, but rarely impulsive. He is proud of Elara’s position and attentive to what the Caldarion alliance means for Thalorin’s future.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Lady Ysara Thalorin**: Wife; the warmer, more openly healing-centered half of their partnership.
- **Lady Elara Caldarion**: Daughter. Proud of her marriage and status, though he may worry that Blackspire’s severity has claimed more of her than he expected.
- **Lord Elias Thalorin**: Son and political face of the next generation.
- **Lucian Caldarion**: Maternal grandson and future of the Caldarion alliance. Halric regards him with a mixture of respect, interest, and political calculation.

**Role in Story:**  
Maternal-line grandfather and the practical face of House Thalorin. Useful for alliance politics, soft-power contrast with Blackspire, and pressure or support connected to Elara’s children.
