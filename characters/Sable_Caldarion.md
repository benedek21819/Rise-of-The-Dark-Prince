# Lady Sable Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lady Sable Caldarion  
**Titles:** Second Concubine of Lord Vesper Caldarion, Mother of Riven and Thorne  
**Status:** Alive

**Appearance:**  
Less refined than Mira or Lady Elara, but naturally beautiful in a more earthy and approachable way. She does not put as much effort into formal presentation and often appears more relaxed or practical in her clothing and demeanor.

**Personality:**  
Warm, straightforward, and practical. She is less concerned with politics and status than Mira. She is deeply devoted to her twin sons and takes pride in their physical strength and loyalty to Lucian. She has a more grounded, almost commoner-like energy compared to the rest of the household, which makes her stand out in the otherwise highly disciplined and elegant environment of House Caldarion.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Riven and Thorne Caldarion**: Very affectionate and protective toward her twin sons. She is proud of their martial talent and their loyalty to Lucian.
- **Lord Vesper Caldarion**: Accepts her position without much political ambition and maintains a relatively uncomplicated relationship with him.
- Generally stays out of deeper household power dynamics and avoids conflict with Lady Elara and Lady Mira.

**Strengths:**
- Genuine warmth and practicality
- Strong maternal devotion
- Lack of political scheming makes her relatively safe within the household

**Flaws:**
- Lacks refinement and political awareness
- Can be overlooked or underestimated by more ambitious members of the house
- Her straightforward nature may leave her unprepared for more complex intrigues

**Role in Story:**  
Provides contrast to the more refined women of House Caldarion. She represents a warmer, simpler maternal presence and helps explain the twins’ straightforward, martial personalities. Her lack of ambition reinforces the internal hierarchy of the household and keeps the focus of power clearly on the main line.
