# Lady Sable Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lady Sable Caldarion  
**Titles:** Second Concubine of Lord Vesper Caldarion, Mother of Kael and Thorne  
**Status:** Alive

**Appearance:** Less refined but naturally beautiful.

**Personality:** Blend of practical and affectionate traits. She is devoted to her sons and supports the family in her own way.

**Key Relationships:**
- Mother of the twins Lord Kael and Lord Thorne Caldarion.
- Concubine in the household.

**Role in Story:** Adds depth to the concubine dynamics in House Caldarion. Her sons' loyalty to Lucian reinforces the house's internal hierarchy and support structure.
