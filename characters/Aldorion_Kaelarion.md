# King Aldorion Kaelarion

**Full Name:** King Aldorion Kaelarion  
**Titles:** Previous King / Previous Head of House Kaelarion  
**Status:** Deceased (previous generation)

**Appearance:** Bright blonde hair and blue eyes with an average physique. He carried himself with natural charisma and regal presence rather than raw physical power. His features are reflected in both his son Aldric and especially in his grandson Dorian and granddaughter Seren.

**Personality:** The Charismatic Visionary — a warm, persuasive, and idealistic leader who believed a strong alliance with House Caldarion could bring lasting peace and stability after years of conflict. He was skilled at inspiring loyalty through vision and personal connection rather than fear or force. His friendship with Lord Ravorn Caldarion was genuine and deep — they balanced each other perfectly, with Aldorion providing the political vision and Ravorn providing the military strength.

**Strengths:** Charisma, political vision, ability to unite people, genuine personal loyalty.
**Flaws:** Perhaps overly idealistic about how long the carefully balanced alliance would remain stable.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Lord Ravorn Caldarion**: His closest friend, almost a brother. Together they reinforced the long-standing alliance between House Kaelarion and House Caldarion that later secured the throne for Aldorion’s line during the Last Continental War.
- **King Aldric Kaelarion** (son): Raised him with a strong sense of duty and the importance of the Caldarion alliance. Aldric’s cautious and stability-focused nature is partly shaped by his father’s vision of lasting peace.

**Legacy:**
Aldorion is remembered as one of the two founding pillars of the current dynasty. His personal bond with Ravorn Caldarion gave the alliance between the two houses a deep emotional foundation that still influences the present. While Ravorn is remembered as the legendary warrior, Aldorion is remembered as the visionary statesman who turned that friendship into a lasting political order.

**Role in Story:** A legendary previous-generation figure whose idealistic vision and genuine friendship with Ravorn created the current power structure. His legacy adds emotional weight to the growing tensions between the two houses in the present day.
