# Lady Elara Caldrith

**Full Name:** Lady Elara Caldrith  
**Titles:** Main Wife of Lord Vesper Caldrith

**Appearance:** Tall and graceful with bright silver hair, pale skin, and sharp, observant eyes. Dresses in elegant black and silver with subtle crimson details.

**Personality:** The Silver Matriarch — politically astute, subtly manipulative, but openly affectionate toward her children. She maintains perfect composure in public while quietly influencing events behind the scenes.

**Strengths:** Political insight, emotional intelligence, fierce maternal protectiveness, excellent network of information.
**Flaws:** Can be overly calculating or struggle with Vesper’s emotional distance.

**Role in Story:** Emotional anchor for Lucian and quiet political influencer. She provides guidance and protection for her children.
