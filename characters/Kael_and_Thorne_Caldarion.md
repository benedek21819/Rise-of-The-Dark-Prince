# Lord Riven and Lord Thorne Caldarion

**Full Names:** Lord Riven Caldarion and Lord Thorne Caldarion  
**Titles:** Younger half-brothers of Lucian (sons of the second concubine, Lady Sable)  
**Birth Year:** 1040 IE  
**Age:** 2 years younger than Lucian (15 years old in present day ≈ 1055 IE)  
**Status:** Alive (twins)

**Appearance:**  
Both have strong, well-built physiques for their age. They share the pale skin and dark hair of House Caldarion, but their features are more rugged and athletic compared to Lucian’s refined elegance. They are often seen in practical training clothes rather than formal attire. While difficult for outsiders to tell apart at first glance, family members can distinguish them easily.

**Shared Personality:**  
Energetic, straightforward, and competitive. They are the “musclehead” type — not particularly academic or politically minded. Both possess extraordinary natural talent for combat and physical training, but almost no talent for magic or Veilweaving. They are highly loyal to Lucian and openly respect him as the future head of the house. They train hard with the clear goal of becoming strong enough to support and protect him. Their thinking is simple: strength, loyalty, and family matter most.

**Distinction Between the Twins:**

- **Riven**: Slightly more hot-headed and impulsive. Prefers aggressive, overwhelming fighting styles. Louder and more expressive. Openly admires Lucian and is eager to impress him.
- **Thorne**: Slightly more steady and observant. More technical and disciplined in combat. Quieter and speaks less. His devotion to Lucian is quieter but just as deep.

**Key Relationships:**
- Strong respect and loyalty toward **Lucian** as the next head and future leader of the family. They see themselves as his future swords and shields.
- Sons of **Lady Sable** (second concubine).
- Generally get along well with their other siblings, though they are closest to each other and to Lucian.

**Strengths:**
- Exceptional physical talent and combat potential
- High endurance and pain tolerance
- Unwavering loyalty
- Excellent coordination with each other in combat

**Flaws:**
- Limited intellectual and magical ability
- Can be reckless (especially Riven)
- Struggle with complex political or social situations
- Tend to solve problems with force first

**Role in Story:**  
They represent the loyal martial backbone of the younger generation of House Caldarion. In Arc 1 they serve as training partners, emotional contrast (simple loyalty vs Lucian’s complexity), and later as reliable combat support. Their lack of political ambition makes them safe and trustworthy in Lucian’s eyes.
