# Lord Kael and Lord Thorne Caldarion

**Full Names:** Lord Kael Caldarion and Lord Thorne Caldarion  
**Titles:** Younger half-brothers of Lucian (sons of the second concubine, Lady Sable)  
**Status:** Alive (twins)

**Appearance:** Strong physique.

**Personality:** Extraordinary talent in combat but no talent for magic. Not particularly smart — more of the "musclehead" type. They are energetic and straightforward.

**Key Relationships:**
- Strong respect for Lucian as the next head and future leader of the family.
- Train hard to become strong support for him.
- Sons of Lady Sable (second concubine).

**Role in Story:** Provide loyal, combat-focused support for Lucian. They represent the more straightforward, physical side of House Caldarion and can serve as reliable (if not always the brightest) allies in battle or training scenarios.
