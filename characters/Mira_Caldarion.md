# Lady Mira Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lady Mira Caldarion  
**Titles:** First Concubine of Lord Vesper Caldarion, Mother of Lyra  
**Status:** Alive

**Appearance:**  
Elegant and refined. She carries herself with grace and composure. Her beauty is polished rather than wild — the kind that fits naturally into the high standards of House Caldarion. She dresses well and maintains a dignified presence.

**Personality:**  
Composed, intelligent, and socially aware. She understands her position as a concubine and navigates the household with careful dignity. She is proud of her daughter Lyra and quietly ambitious for her future, even if she herself does not push aggressively. She is more politically minded than Sable and better at reading the atmosphere of the house.

**Key Relationships:**
- **Lady Lyra Caldarion**: Deeply attached to her daughter and supportive of her. She takes quiet pride in Lyra’s grace and potential.
- **Lord Vesper Caldarion**: Maintains a proper, respectful, and non-confrontational relationship with him.
- **Lady Elara Caldarion** (main wife): Respectful and somewhat distant. She is careful not to overstep.
- **Lucian Caldarion**: Treats him with correct courtesy, fully aware that he is the heir of the house.

**Strengths:**
- Social grace and political awareness
- Composure and dignity
- Ability to navigate the complex household hierarchy

**Flaws:**
- Can be overly cautious
- Her quiet ambitions for Lyra may eventually create tension if a political marriage is pushed

**Role in Story:**  
Represents the more refined, politically aware side of the concubine system within House Caldarion. She adds subtle tension regarding Lyra’s value as a potential political marriage candidate and provides contrast to the more grounded Lady Sable.
