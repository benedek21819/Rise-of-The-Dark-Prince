# Crown Prince Dorian Kaelarion

**Full Name:** Crown Prince Dorian Kaelarion  
**Titles:** Heir to the Throne of the Kingdom  
**Age:** 1 year older than Lucian Caldrith

**Appearance:** Handsome but softer and less commanding than Lucian. He has blonde hair and blue eyes, and is slightly shorter than Lucian. His features are refined and aristocratic, but he lacks the cold intensity and natural presence of the Dark Prince.

**Personality:** A blend of the Insecure Schemer and the Entitled Heir. Dorian is ambitious, jealous, and deeply insecure about living in the shadow of both his father and House Caldrith’s overwhelming military power. He believes the throne is his birthright and resents how dependent the royal family has become on House Caldrith. Cowardly in direct confrontation, he prefers to scheme from the shadows and manipulate others to do his dirty work. He is easily flattered and can be swayed by those who promise him greater power or independence.

**Strengths:** Political cunning, ability to form temporary alliances, moderate talent in both magic and combat, understanding of court intrigue.
**Flaws:** Cowardice, jealousy, emotional volatility, poor long-term judgment, tendency to underestimate Lucian.

**Abilities:** Moderate talent for both magic and combat.

**Goals:**
- Reduce House Caldrith’s influence over the crown
- Secure greater personal power and independence as the future king

**Key Relationships:**
- **Lucian Caldrith**: Sees him as a dangerous rival and a threat to his future authority. Actively works to undermine him and House Caldrith.
- **Princess Seren Kaelarion** (younger sister): Protective of her, but also resentful of her closeness to Lucian and the political engagement.
- **King Aldric Kaelarion** (father): Strained relationship. Dorian wants more power and independence from his father’s cautious leadership.

**Role in Story:** Primary internal antagonist within the royal family. His schemes against House Caldrith and Lucian create ongoing political tension and conflict.
