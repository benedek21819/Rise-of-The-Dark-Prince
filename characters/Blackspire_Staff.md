# Blackspire Keep – Household Staff

Key supporting characters who live and work at Blackspire Keep.

---

### Serris – Steward of Blackspire

**Role:** Overall management of the household, supplies, servants, and daily operations of the keep.  
**Personality:** Extremely competent, dry, and slightly weary. He has served House Caldarion for many years and is used to the cold, demanding nature of the family. Loyal and highly efficient, but quietly careful around Lord Vesper and Lucian. He knows many small practical secrets of the keep and is trusted with sensitive logistical matters.  
**Notes:** One of the most important non-family figures in the daily running of Blackspire.

---

### Martha – Head Housekeeper / Senior Maid

**Role:** Oversees the maids, cleaning, and the internal order of the living quarters.  
**Personality:** Strict, efficient, and no-nonsense. She has served the family for decades and maintains high standards. Beneath her stern exterior she is quietly fond of the children, especially Selene, though she rarely shows it openly. One of the few people who can openly scold the younger members of the family without fear.  
**Notes:** Represents continuity and old household discipline.

---

### Cole – Personal Attendant to Lucian

**Role:** Assists Lucian with clothing, personal preparation, and daily needs. Often coordinates with Lyra regarding Lucian’s appearance.  
**Personality:** Quiet, highly attentive, and discreet. He is somewhat intimidated by Lucian’s cold reputation but remains professional and loyal. He speaks only when necessary and is extremely careful with anything related to the heir.  
**Notes:** A small but consistent presence in Lucian’s daily life. Useful for showing Lucian’s private routines.

---

### Bram – Head Cook

**Role:** Runs the kitchens of Blackspire. Responsible for feeding the family, the Silver Guard, and the rest of the household.  
**Personality:** Practical, straightforward, and one of the warmest ordinary people in the keep. He is loud in the kitchens and brings a rare sense of ordinary human energy into Blackspire. The twins and Selene are often drawn to the kitchens because of him.  
**Notes:** Provides contrast to the cold atmosphere of the rest of the household.

---

### Fenn – Senior Stable Master

**Role:** Oversees the ordinary mounts and assists with the practical needs of the dragons when required (under strict supervision).  
**Personality:** Weathered, calm, and used to dangerous creatures. He speaks little and does not waste words. Respected by the guards and trusted with the more hazardous animal-related duties of the keep.  
**Notes:** Grounded, practical presence connected to the physical side of Blackspire’s power.

---

### Lina – Young Maid

**Role:** One of the ordinary maids, frequently assigned to duties in the family wing.  
**Personality:** Nervous but kind. She is somewhat afraid of Lucian’s cold reputation and the overall severity of the house, but she is warmer toward Selene and Lyra. Represents the perspective of an ordinary person living under the shadow of House Caldarion.  
**Notes:** Useful for showing how the family is viewed by common staff and for lighter or more human moments.

---

*These characters help make Blackspire feel lived-in and give Lucian’s home environment more texture. They can be expanded further as needed.*
