# Lady Lyra Caldarion

**Full Name:** Lady Lyra Caldarion  
**Titles:** Older half-sister of Lucian (daughter of the first concubine, Lady Mira)  
**Status:** Alive

**Appearance:** Smart and fashionable. She has a good sense for style and likes to dress up.

**Personality:** Warm, responsible, and caring. As the oldest sister, she feels it is her duty to look after her younger siblings. She has no ambition for power and simply wants a happy life supporting the family. She is intelligent and practical.

**Key Relationships:**
- Strong affectionate relationship with Lucian (she feels protective of him as the heir).
- Close with her younger siblings.
- Daughter of Lady Mira (first concubine).

**Role in Story:** Provides emotional support and a more grounded, affectionate perspective within the family. She acts as a stabilizing, caring older sister figure for Lucian and the younger children.
