# Lady Lyra Caldrith

**Full Name:** Lady Lyra Caldrith  
**Titles:** Older half-sister of Lucian Caldrith

**Appearance:** Long dark hair with silver streaks, sharp features, and elegant beauty. Dresses in black and silver with a refined style.

**Personality:** Warm, responsible, and affectionate. As the oldest sister, she feels a strong duty to care for her younger siblings. She has no personal ambition for power — she simply wants a happy, stable life supporting the family.

**Strengths:** Smart, socially perceptive, excellent sense of fashion and presentation.
**Role in Story:** Supportive older sister figure who provides emotional stability and affection within the complex Caldrith family. She has a particularly strong bond with Lucian.
