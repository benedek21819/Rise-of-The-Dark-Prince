## Dragons

Dragons are extremely rare and immensely powerful creatures. Currently, only **6 dragons** are publicly known to exist on the continent.

- Dragons grow stronger as they age, both physically and in destructive capability.
- They can be **bonded** to specific individuals (almost always high-ranking nobles).
- A bonded dragon can be ridden.
- If the bond between dragon and rider is strong enough, they can sense each other’s emotions.

### Current Known Distribution
- **House Caldrith**: 2 dragons (including the current largest and strongest dragon on the continent)
- **Royal Family**: 1 dragon
- **Other Great Houses**: 1 dragon each in three different houses

Dragons represent a massive strategic, military, and symbolic advantage. Their ownership heavily influences the balance of power among the Great Houses.
