## Key Historical Events (Chronological)

### The Bond Between the Previous Generation
The father of King Aldric Kaelarion and the father of Lord Vesper Caldrith (Lucian’s grandfather) shared a very strong, brother-like friendship. Their deep personal bond was the foundation of the political alliance between House Kaelarion and House Caldrith. Together, they arranged the political marriage and mutual support that later allowed House Kaelarion to claim the throne with Caldrith’s decisive military backing during the Last Continental War.

Lucian’s grandfather (the previous head of House Caldrith) was the last rider of the hidden dragon **Morvath**. He died in the Last Continental War, and it was publicly claimed that Morvath died with him. In reality, Morvath survived and has been kept hidden by House Caldrith ever since.

### The Last Continental War
A catastrophic war in which nearly every major and minor house fought for power and resources. The victory of House Kaelarion, secured through the crucial support of House Caldrith, established the current royal dynasty and the present balance of power.
