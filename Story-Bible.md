## How to Use This Bible
1. Start with the **Logline & Synopsis** to ground the core story.
2. Develop **Characters** early — they drive everything.
3. Build **World & Magic** rules before writing major scenes.
4. Keep the **Timeline** updated to avoid continuity errors.
5. Use **Notes & Ideas** for brainstorming without polluting other sections.

## Maintenance Rules
- Always update the relevant file when adding new lore, characters, or plot points.
- Cross-reference between sections (e.g. link characters to locations).
- Review before starting a new writing session or chapter.
