## Current Chapters / Episodes

### Opening Scene / Chapter 1: The Remnant in the Garden
**Summary:** Lucian walks through the gardens of Blackspire Keep with his father, Lord Vesper. They come upon the remnants of an old stone gate, covered in moss and partially ruined. Carved upon it is a radiant Black Sun — the rays and disk made from deep dark stone. Lucian asks what it is. Vesper replies that it is merely “a remnant of the past,” explaining that it was part of the earliest structures built by their family. Nothing more. The conversation moves on, leaving the symbol unexplained and the moment deceptively quiet.

**Key Events:**
- Lucian and Vesper walking in the gardens of Blackspire.
- Discovery of the moss-covered, ruined stone gate.
- Carving of the radiant Black Sun (deep dark stone).
- Lucian’s question and Vesper’s deliberate downplaying of its significance.

**Character Development:** Establishes the quiet, measured relationship between father and son. Shows Vesper’s habit of withholding deeper truths. Plants the Black Sun as an early visual motif that will later become critically important.

**Cliffhanger / Hook:** The casual dismissal of something that is clearly ancient and significant creates quiet curiosity. Readers are left wondering what the symbol truly means.

**Word Count / Page Estimate:** Short opening scene / early chapter.
